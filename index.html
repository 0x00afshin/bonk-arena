Frequently Asked Questions

What is Balls.fun?➤
Balls.fun is a high-stakes, real-time multiplayer game on the Solana blockchain. Players bid with $BONK to control a "ball," and the goal is to consume smaller players to grow bigger and claim their BONK.
How does the BONK burn work?➤
A total of 10% is deducted from every bid. 5% of this is permanently burned (removed from circulation), which helps make $BONK more scarce. The other 5% is sent to a marketing wallet to fund community growth and partnerships.
Is the game fair?➤
Yes. The game logic is handled by on-chain smart contracts on Solana. Your starting size is directly proportional to your bid, and all interactions are governed by the rules of the contract.
What happens if I get consumed?➤
If your ball is consumed by a larger player, you lose the $BONK you bid with for that round. The winner who consumed you claims a portion of it. It's a high-risk, high-reward game.
